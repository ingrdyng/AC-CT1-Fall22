Multi-Channel Music Controller
Button Beats
Process: A study to understand the visualization of sensory and sound. I wanted to take the opportunity to create an environment where a touch of a button can trigger a sound while using LED lights to mirror the reverberance of said sound. The current form is meant to be a low-fidelity prototype of a launchpad for personal studies of understanding the form, funciton, technicality, and use of light visualization for the experience of users.
